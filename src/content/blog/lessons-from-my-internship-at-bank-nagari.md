During my internship, I worked on an internal system for registration, placement, and attendance tracking.

Three lessons stood out:

## 1) Internal tools need excellent UX too

People use internal systems every day. Small UX improvements reduce mistakes and save hours.

## 2) Clear ownership beats perfect architecture

In small teams, shipping depends on fast decisions. Define who owns each area and keep communication short.

## 3) Documentation is a force multiplier

Simple docs for setup, deployment, and conventions helped new contributors ramp up faster.

This project taught me that reliability and clarity often matter more than flashy features.
